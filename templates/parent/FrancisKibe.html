Francis Kibe's Family

Francis Kibe was the first Born to Bernard Karura and Ruth Nyakinyi and was born in Kiamworia. He was a teacher by proffession. He married Grace Waithira and together they were blessed with seven children. He passed away and he is buried in Kiamworia Kiambu where he had built his home.
