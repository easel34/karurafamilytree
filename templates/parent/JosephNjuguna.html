Joseph Njuguna's Family

Joseph Njuguna was the son to Bernard and Ruth. He was blessed with four children ruth jackline Nyakinyi, Agnes Abigael Mbaire, the late Frida Agnes Mbaire & the late Kevas Bernard Karura. Joseph Njuguna worked in the meterological department.
